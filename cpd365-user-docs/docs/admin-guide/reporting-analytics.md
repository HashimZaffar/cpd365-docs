# **Reports & Analytics**

The **Reports & Analytics** dashboard gives Organization Admins and Team Managers a complete view of learner activity, compliance status, and overall CPD performance across their teams or entire organization.

This is your central hub for:

* Monitoring progress toward mandatory training
* Tracking CPD hours across departments
* Identifying overdue or at-risk learners
* Exporting audit-ready reports
* Reviewing program-level outcomes

The reporting suite is optimized for clarity, accuracy, and healthcare compliance workflows.

## **Exports & Audit Tools**

Exports include:

* CPD hour summaries

### Audit-Ready Layout

PDF exports follow healthcare compliance requirements with:

* Organization name and logo

* Summary metrics
* Table of evidence
* Timestamp & export ID

## **Data Retention**

Reporting data is retained according to:

* Organization policy
* Regulatory requirements
* CPD365 internal retention policies

Most organizations choose:

* **7 years** for audit-related data
* **Indefinite** for certificate IDs (verification purposes)

## **Troubleshooting Reporting Issues**

### Report not loading?

* Check date filters
* Ensure course visibility hasn‚Äôt been restricted
* Refresh the dashboard

### Missing learners?

* Ensure they belong to the selected team
* Confirm they are active users
* Check if they were added after assignment creation

### CSV missing columns?

* Ensure you selected the full export, not a filtered subset

### Charts look incorrect?

* Try resetting all filters
* Confirm the reporting period aligns with your CPD cycle
