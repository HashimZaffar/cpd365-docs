# **Certificates & CPD Tracking**

CPD365 automatically records every course you complete and generates a CPD certificate you can download, store, or share with your workplace.
Your CPD hours are then added to your personal **CPD Portfolio**, keeping you audit-ready at all times.

This page explains how certificates are created, where to find them, how CPD hours are recorded, and how to export your full CPD history.

---

## **How Certificates Work**

Once you complete a course:

1. All modules are marked complete
2. You pass the final quiz
3. A background process generates your certificate
4. Your CPD hours are added to your portfolio
5. You receive a completion notification

Certificates typically generate **within 5‚Äì10 seconds**.

Each certificate includes:

* Your full name
* Course title
* CPD hours earned
* Completion date
* Instructor name
* Unique certificate ID (for verification)
* CPD365 branding and validation seal

## **Downloading Your Certificate**

You can download a certificate from anywhere it appears:

### **Option 1: From the Completed Course Page**

1. Open the course you completed
2. Click **Download Certificate**
3. Save the PDF

### **Option 2: From Your Dashboard**

1. Scroll to the **Completed** section
2. Select the course
3. Click **Certificate**

### **Option 3: From Your CPD Portfolio**

1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí My CPD**
2. Find the course in the log
3. Click **Download Certificate**

!!! warning
Some workplaces require certificates to be uploaded into internal systems.
Always keep a second copy saved in your personal files.

## **Understanding Your CPD Portfolio**

Your **CPD Portfolio** is a centralized record of all CPD activities completed on CPD365.

It includes:

* Total CPD hours earned this registration year
* List of all completed courses
* Dates completed
* Download links for certificates
* Notes and reflections (if added)
* Filters and export tools

This portfolio is automatically maintained and always up to date.

## **CPD Hours Explained**

Each CPD365 course lists the number of hours you will earn upon completion.
Examples:

* Short course ‚Üí **0.5 to 1 CPD hour**
* Intermediate course ‚Üí **1.5 to 2 CPD hours**
* Full competency programs ‚Üí **3+ CPD hours**

### How CPD Hours Are Calculated

Hours are based on:

* Estimated lesson duration
* Interactive components
* Required readings
* Clinical scenario time

These values are aligned with standard CPD frameworks used by Australian healthcare regulators.

!!! note
CPD365 logs only the hours earned from your completed CPD365 courses.
You may record external CPD separately (if supported by your organization).

## **Exporting Your CPD Log**

You can export your full CPD record for annual renewal or workplace audits.

Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí My CPD ‚Üí Export** and choose:

* **CSV** (for spreadsheets)
* **PDF** (formatted for audits)

Exports include:

* All completed courses
* CPD hours
* Completion dates
* Certificate IDs
* Reflection notes (if enabled)

!!! tip
PDF exports are structured in a clean, audit-ready format for AHPRA and internal compliance teams.

## **Reflection Notes (Optional)**

Some organizations require reflective practice notes.
CPD365 supports this by allowing learners to attach notes to courses.

Reflection notes may include:

* Key takeaways
* How the learning applies to your practice
* Areas for further learning
* Impact on patient care

These notes appear in your CPD Portfolio and export files.

## **Troubleshooting Certificates**

### Certificate not generated?

* Ensure the course is fully completed
* Confirm the final quiz is passed
* Refresh the page after a few seconds

### Incorrect name on certificate?

Update your profile under **Settings**, then regenerate the certificate by reopening the course.

### Certificate won‚Äôt download?

* Check your pop-up settings
* Try a different browser
* Ensure your device has storage available

If issues persist, contact Support.
