# **Profile & Settings**

Your **Profile & Settings** page lets you manage your personal information, profession, registration details, notifications, and account preferences.

This page walks you through every setting available to learners on CPD365 and explains how each option impacts your experience.

---

## **Accessing Your Profile**

To open your Profile settings:

1. Click your **avatar** or **name** in the top-right corner
2. Select **Profile & Settings**

You’ll land on a clean, tabbed interface that organizes settings into manageable sections — following **Progressive Disclosure** to reduce cognitive load.

---

## **Profile Overview**

Your Profile consists of four main areas:

* **Personal Information**
* **Professional Details**
* **Notifications & Preferences**
* **Security Settings**

Each section is intentionally structured to allow quick scanning with minimal decision-making (Hick’s Law).

---

## **Personal Information**

This section contains your core account details.

You can update:

* First name
* Last name
* Email address
* Profile picture
* Preferred display name

### Changing your email?

You’ll be prompted to:

1. Enter your new email
2. Confirm your password
3. Verify the new email address

!!! note
Updating your email immediately updates the email shown on new certificates.

---

## **Professional Details**

Tailored for healthcare professionals, this section lets you define your clinical identity.

You can edit:

* Profession

  * Registered Nurse (RN)
  * Enrolled Nurse (EN)
  * Midwife
  * NDIS Support Worker
  * Aged Care Worker
  * Student/Graduate
* Specialty / Clinical Area
  (e.g., Emergency, Paediatrics, Mental Health, Community)
* Registration number (optional)
  (e.g., AHPRA number)
* Workplace or organization name

These details help CPD365:

* Recommend relevant courses
* Tailor your dashboard
* Add appropriate metadata to certificates
* Align CPD suggestions with your clinical practice

---

## **Notifications & Preferences**

This section controls how and when CPD365 communicates with you.

### Email Notification Options

Toggle notifications for:

* **New course assignments**
* **Certificate availability**
* **CPD summary reminders**
* **New course releases**
* **System updates**

All toggles use large, mobile-friendly switches (Fitts’s Law) to reduce tap/click friction.

### Reminders

Set reminder frequency for:

* In-progress courses
* Approaching CPD deadlines
* Annual renewal cycle

!!! tip
Enabling renewal reminders helps ensure you meet your professional CPD requirements on time.

---

## **Security Settings**

Designed around **User Control & Freedom**, these settings give you full ownership of your account.

### Change Password

You can update your password anytime by:

1. Entering your current password
2. Creating a new secure password
3. Confirming the change

Password criteria follow security best practices and are displayed inline.

### Two-Factor Authentication (If enabled)

You may see an option to enable 2FA via:

* SMS
* Authentication apps (e.g., Google Authenticator)

This enhances account security, especially if your employer requires it.

---

## **Timezone & Locale**

These settings allow CPD365 to display:

* Correct course due dates
* Local times for reminder emails
* Region-specific CPD rules (in future releases)

Default selections are based on your browser’s region.

---

## **Accessibility Settings (Optional)**

Some users will see accessibility preferences such as:

* High-contrast mode
* Larger text mode
* Reduced motion mode (limits GIF autoplay)
* Caption preferences for videos

These options follow WCAG guidelines and support a wider range of learners, especially in healthcare environments with varied device access.

---

## **Data & Privacy Controls**

CPD365 gives learners transparent access to their information.

### Download Your Data

Export your:

* Profile info
* Course history
* CPD portfolio
* Certificates

### Delete Account

You can request account deletion. This will:

* Remove personal data
* Revoke login access
* Retain anonymized learning analytics (for platform metrics)

!!! warning
Account deletion is permanent. Certificates cannot be recovered afterward.

---

## **Common Actions**

### Update Your Name

1. Go to **Profile & Settings → Personal Information**
2. Edit your name fields
3. Save changes
4. Reopen completed courses to regenerate updated certificates

### Change Your Profession

1. Go to **Professional Details**
2. Select your new profession
3. CPD recommendations refresh automatically

### Stop Receiving Emails

1. Go to **Notifications & Preferences**
2. Toggle off the emails you want to disable

### Change Password

1. Go to **Security**
2. Enter current password
3. Set a new one

---

## **Troubleshooting Profile Issues**

### Can't update email?

* Ensure the new email isn’t already in use
* Check for typos
* Confirm your password
* Verify the link sent to your new email

### Changes not appearing in certificates?

Certificates reflect profile details **at the time of download**.
Re-download them after updating your profile.

### Cannot upload a profile photo?

* Max file size: 2MB
* Supported formats: JPG, PNG
* Ensure cookies/storage aren’t blocked
