## **Step 2: View a Course Details Page**

Click any course to open its **Course Details** page. You‚Äôll see:

* **Course title and summary**
* **CPD hours earned** upon completion
* **Difficulty level** (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
* **Learning outcomes**
* **Instructor**
* **Preview video** (if available)
* **Modules included**

!!! tip
Each course includes a short summary to help you decide if it matches your learning goals for your CPD plan.

---

## **Step 3: Enrol in the Course**

To begin learning:

1. Click the **Enrol** button.
2. The course will move to your **In Progress** section on the dashboard.
3. Click **Start Course** to open the learning player.

## **Step 4: Complete the Course Modules**

Courses are made up of **modules**, which may include:

* Short video lessons
* Clinical scenarios
* Interactive questions
* PDF resources or guidelines
* Reflective practice prompts

The learning player shows:

* **Left panel:** Module list
* **Center:** Video or lesson content
* **Right panel:** Notes, attachments, transcripts

### Your progress saves automatically

If you close the browser, CPD365 will pick up exactly where you left off.

!!! note
Some lessons require you to view at least 90% of the content before they can be marked as complete.

---

## **Step 5: Complete the Quiz**

Most courses include a short quiz at the end.

* Passing score: **usually 70% or higher**
* Unlimited retakes (unless restricted by your organization)
* Feedback is shown after each attempt to support learning

!!! tip
Quizzes are designed to reinforce key clinical concepts‚Äînot trick you.

---

## **Step 6: Mark the Course as Complete**

Once all modules and the quiz are finished:

* Your **Course Status** changes to **Completed**
* A certificate is automatically generated
* CPD hours are added to your **CPD Portfolio**

## **Step 7: Download Your Certificate**

Your CPD365 certificate includes:

* Your full name
* Course title
* CPD hours earned
* Completion date
* Unique certificate ID

To download:

1. Open the course you completed
2. Click **Download Certificate**
3. Save the PDF to your device

!!! warning
If your organization requires certificates for compliance, always download and store your PDF in your workplace system as well.

---
