# **Sign Up & Login**

Creating an account on CPD365 takes less than a minute. Once signed in, you can browse the course catalog, enrol in CPD activities, and begin tracking your annual CPD hours.

This guide will walk you through the full process for:

* Creating a new learner account
* Verifying your email
* Logging in
* Resetting your password
* Troubleshooting common sign-in issues

---

## **Create a New CPD365 Account**

Follow the steps below to set up your learner account:

1. **Open CPD365**
   Navigate to the CPD365 homepage and click **Sign Up** in the top-right corner.

2. **Enter your details**
   You’ll be asked for:

   * Full name
   * Email address
   * Password
   * Profession (e.g., RN, EN, Midwife, NDIS Worker)
   * Registration number (optional, e.g., AHPRA)

3. **Accept the Terms**
   Tick the checkbox to accept CPD365’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

4. **Click “Create Account”**

5. **Verify your email**
   You’ll receive an email with a verification link.
   Click the link to activate your account.

!!! tip
Can’t find the verification email?
Check your spam folder or search for “Welcome to CPD365”.

---

## **Sign In to Your Account**

Once your account is verified:

1. Go to the **Login** page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Log In**

After logging in, you’ll be taken directly to your **Learner Dashboard**.

### **Optional: Single Sign-On (SSO)**

If enabled for your organization, you may also see:

* **Sign in with Google**
* **Sign in with Microsoft**

These options allow you to access CPD365 without a separate password.

---

## **Password Requirements**

For security and compliance, your password must include:

* At least **8 characters**
* At least **1 uppercase letter**
* At least **1 lowercase letter**
* At least **1 number**

!!! warning
Avoid using shared or workplace-generic passwords.
Your CPD records contain personal and professional data.

---

## **Reset Your Password**

If you forget your password:

1. On the Login page, click **Forgot Password?**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link
4. Click the link and set a new password

Password resets typically take effect immediately.

!!! tip
Still can’t access your account?
Try using the email address originally used to sign up. Many users attempt to log in with a secondary work email by mistake.

---

## **Troubleshooting Login Problems**

Below are the most common issues and how to fix them:

### ❌ **“Email not recognized”**

* Check spelling
* Ensure you’re using the email you signed up with
* If your organization migrated accounts, confirm account activation

### ❌ **“Incorrect password”**

* Use the **Forgot Password** flow to reset
* Ensure Caps Lock is off

### ❌ **Verification email didn’t arrive**

* Check Spam/Junk
* Ensure your inbox isn’t full
* Click **Resend Verification Email** on the login page

### ❌ **Account locked**

For security, accounts lock after multiple failed attempts.
Locked accounts automatically unlock after **15 minutes**, or you can contact support.
